Exploring Andrew's Work

In addition to discussing Andrew's favourite songs, we also asked him to select a handful of songs from his own discography that have been particularly meaningful to him and his musical journey...

Jay Z

The Black Album

"99 Problems"

-- RELEASED 2003

This song marks one of the first times I used parallel compression in a mix. I bounced many of the tracks through a Fatso that we had in the studio, and then brought the parallel tracks in and out to make things get bigger and smaller throughout the song. Other than that, the mix is ridiculously simple. We used 24 inputs on a Neve 8068, one 1176 on Jay's vocal, and no automation.
